How We Score Hosting

📡
Uptime (30%)
Measured with UptimeRobot at 1-minute intervals over 90 days. Industry minimum is 99.9%. We penalize anything below 99.95% significantly.
⚡
Speed/TTFB (25%)
Time to First Byte measured from 5 global locations using GTmetrix and Pingdom on identical WordPress test sites. Under 200ms is excellent.
🎧
Support (20%)
40+ live chat and email interactions per provider, rated on response time, technical accuracy, and problem resolution rate.
💰
Pricing Fairness (15%)
Ratio of introductory to renewal pricing, hidden fees, cancellation policy. We heavily penalize misleading pricing practices.

About HostingJoy

We Built the Review Site
We Wished Existed

HostingJoy was started after one too many experiences with "best hosting" articles that ranked providers based on who paid the biggest commission — not who actually performed best. We decided to do it differently.

Our Story

In 2020, after paying for three different hosting providers in six months — each recommended by a different "top 10 hosting" article — and experiencing poor uptime, slow speeds, and useless support on all three, we decided to do our own research the right way.

We bought hosting plans with our own money, set up real test sites, and monitored them for 90 days each. What we found was shocking: the most heavily marketed and highest-commission providers consistently underperformed the less-promoted ones.

HostingJoy was built to be the resource we needed back then. Real test data. Real methodology. No paid placements. And the honesty to tell you when a heavily-advertised host isn't worth your money.

Our Testing Methodology

  • Every hosting plan is purchased with HostingJoy's own funds — no free accounts or media access
  • Identical WordPress test sites installed on each provider (same theme, same plugins, same content)
  • 90 days of continuous uptime monitoring via UptimeRobot at 1-minute intervals
  • TTFB measured from 5 global server locations using GTmetrix and WebPageTest
  • 40+ live support interactions per provider, rated on response time and accuracy
  • Renewal pricing and total 3-year cost calculated for every provider
  • Rankings updated quarterly with fresh data — we never let old test data persist indefinitely

Our Pledge

We will never rank a hosting provider higher than it deserves because of commission rates. We will always publish the full renewal pricing alongside introductory offers. We will remove providers from our recommendations when their performance drops — even if they continue paying high commissions.
